## Voice Scoring Rubric

Use this to evaluate if content matches the voice:

| Criteria | Score 1-5 | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Sounds like them | [ ] | Does it authentically sound like the brand? |
| Uses preferred vocabulary | [ ] | Uses words from the 'use' list? |
| Avoids forbidden phrases | [ ] | Avoids words from the 'avoid' list? |
| Matches tone for context | [ ] | Appropriate tone for this communication type? |
| Correct formality level | [ ] | Matches documented formality preferences? |
| Authentic and natural | [ ] | Feels natural, not forced or artificial? |
| **TOTAL** | **[ ]/30** | |

**Scoring guide:**
- 5 = Excellent - Perfectly matches the voice
- 4 = Good - Minor adjustments needed
- 3 = Acceptable - Some revision required
- 2 = Weak - Significant revision needed
- 1 = Poor - Complete rewrite required

**Minimum passing score:** 24/30

Content scoring below 24 must be revised before use.
